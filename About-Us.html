ABOUT US

Dental Care is one of the largest chain of dental clinics in India, operating 100 Clinics in Mumbai, Pune, Surat, Ahmadabad and Bangalore. We have more than 500 expert dentists who provide all possible dental treatments under one roof. With a collective experience of treating more than 10 million patients, Dental Care is an authority in dental treatments and processes. The expertise and authoritativeness of doctors working in Dental Care can be justified by the fact that the doctors at Dental Care have treated more than 10 million patients, conducted more than 1,50,000 Root Canals, more than 160,000 Prosthetics, more than 15,000 implant surgeries and many more dental treatments. With the vast collective experience and expertise of the dentists and doctors at Dental Care, we can very confidently claim thought leadership in the domain of dentistry in India. Our thought leadership, expertise and authority on dental treatments and processes provides us a high level of trustworthiness among our patients.
