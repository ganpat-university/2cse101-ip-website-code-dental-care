What are the common cause of Tooth sensitivity?

According to American dental association (ADA) common cause are:
1.Vigorous brushing
2.Chemical Erosion of teeth
3.Periodontal disease
4.Teeth grinding habit
5.Teeth decay or broken teeth

Reasons for why does your tooth hurt after Root Canal

1). Once the root canal treatment is completed no living pulp tissue remains inside the tooth, but nerve endings remain in the ligaments that attach the tooth to the surrounding bone.

2). One possible cause of pain is inflammation of the periapical tissues post root canal.

3) Another source of post root canal discomfort is a high bite and this can easily be corrected by a dentist.
